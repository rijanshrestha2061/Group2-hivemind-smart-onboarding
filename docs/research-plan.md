# Research Plan — Smart Onboarding

## Project
HiveMind × Ravensbourne — Smart Onboarding

## Research goal
Understand how workplace professionals experience onboarding when starting a new job, team, tool, or process. We want to identify unclear steps, missing information, and support needs.

## Target participants
Professionals with real workplace experience. We will not interview students.

## Planned participants (completed)
- Participant 1: P01 — Deepak Thapa — UX/AI Designer, enterprise SaaS company
- Participant 2: P02 — Bishal Basnet — Software Designer, technology company
- Participant 3: P03 — Vashu (Bashudev Upreti) — Project Leader
- Participant 4: P05 — Dhiraj Mandal — Business Analyst, technology company
- Participant 5: P06 — Shubham Parajuli — IT Support Analyst, financial services
- Participant 6: P07 — Rijan Shrestha — Marketing Coordinator, retail company

## Interview method
Short semi-structured interviews, approximately 30–40 minutes each. All 6 interviews completed and transcribed. Note: participant numbering skips P04 — only 6 interviews were conducted in total (P01, P02, P03, P04, P05, P06).

## Interview topics
- First-day or first-week onboarding experience
- Finding important information
- Understanding what to do next
- Asking for help or finding the right contact
- Useful support tools and improvements

## Interview date and location/method
Conducted individually by each team member between 02 July 2026 and 11 July 2026, via recorded video call, transcribed afterward.

## Notes and consent
Participants were told the interview was for a student UX research project. Verbal consent to record was obtained at the start of each session. All transcripts are anonymised by participant ID (P01–P06); no real names are stored in transcripts.

## Outcome
All 6 transcripts synthesised into `research/synthesis/pain-points.md` and `pain-points-list.md`, feeding into `docs/hypotheses.md` (4 hypotheses) and `design/journey-map/journey-map.md`.
